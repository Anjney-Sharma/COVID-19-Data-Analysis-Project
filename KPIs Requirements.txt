KPI Requirements

1. Total Vaccinations Administered Per Country
-> Definition: Sum of all doses given across each of the selected countries in the chosen time period.

2. People Vaccinated (at least 1 dose)
-> Definition: Total number of individuals who have received at least one dose.

3. Fully Vaccinated People
-> Definition: Total number of individuals who have completed the vaccination schedule.

4. Average Daily Vaccinations
-> Definition: Average number of doses administered per day across all countries in the dataset.

5. Highest Single-Day Vaccination Count (Country)
-> Definition: The highest daily vaccinations achieved by a country in the selected time range.

6. Top Performing Country (by Total Vaccinations)
-> Definition: Country with highest total vaccinations administered.
